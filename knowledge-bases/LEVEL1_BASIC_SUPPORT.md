### Q: What is a "fit score"?

**Answer:**
Fit score (0-100%) shows how well you match a job based on:

**Calculation:**
- Your skills vs. job requirements
- Higher % = more of your skills match
- 80%+ = Strong match
- 50-79% = Good match
- Below 50% = Partial match

**Example:**
- You have: Python, React, AWS (3 skills)
- Job requires: Python, React, Docker (3 skills)
- You match 2 of 3 = 67% fit score

**Tip:** Focus on jobs with 60%+ fit scores for best results!

**Support Levels:** Level 1
